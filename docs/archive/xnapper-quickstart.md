### 1. Access the Tool
- Click on **Screenshot Tool** in the sidebar under "Utilities"
- Or search for "screenshot" in the search bar (Cmd/Ctrl+K)
- Or use the shortcut: **Ctrl+Shift+S**

### 2. Capture a Screenshot
Choose one of three capture modes:
- **Full Screen** - Captures your entire display
- **Window** - Select a specific window to capture
- **Area** - Capture the full screen (cropping coming in Phase 3)

Click "Capture Screenshot" to take the shot.

### 3. Enhance Your Screenshot
- Toggle **Auto-balance** to automatically enhance colors and contrast
- Use zoom controls to inspect details (25% - 300%)
- View image dimensions and file size

### 4. Export Your Screenshot
- Choose format: **PNG** (best quality), **JPG** (smaller size), or **WebP** (modern)
- Adjust quality for JPG/WebP (1-100%)
- Enter a custom filename (optional)
- Click "Save to File" to save or "Copy to Clipboard" to copy
